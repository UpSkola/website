UpSkola Tutor – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 22nd August, 2025

Last Updated: 22nd August, 2025

UpSkola values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information when you use the UpSkola Tutor App (“the App”). By creating an account or using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

When you register and use the App as a tutor, we may collect the following:

Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, date of birth, identification details.

Professional Information: Qualifications, subjects, experience, profile description, and availability.

Financial Information: Bank or mobile money details for payouts.

Usage Data: Login activity, device type, IP address, session history.

Communications: Messages, notifications, or support requests made through the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

Create and manage your tutor account.

Verify your identity and qualifications.

Process payments, commissions, and withdrawals.

Display your profile to potential students.

Communicate with you about updates, payments, and policy changes.

Monitor and improve platform safety, quality, and performance.

Comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your data. However, we may share information with:

Students: Your profile, subjects, and availability (but not sensitive financial details).

Service Providers: Payment processors, banks, mobile money operators, or video call integration partners.

Regulators or Law Enforcement: Where legally required, or if there is suspected fraud, misconduct, or security threats.

UpSkola Affiliates: Entities that help us operate or improve our services.

4. Data Security

We use encryption, authentication, and secure servers to protect your data.

You are responsible for safeguarding your login credentials.

In case of a breach, we will notify you promptly as required by law.

5. Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as your tutor account is active.

After account closure, we may retain certain data for legal, tax, or fraud-prevention purposes (typically up to 3 years).

Session history and payment records may be retained longer if required by law.

6. Your Rights

Under Ghana’s Data Protection Act and other applicable laws, you have the right to:

Access a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

Request deletion of your data (subject to legal and financial record-keeping requirements).

Withdraw consent to data processing (by closing your account).

Lodge complaints with the Data Protection Commission of Ghana.

7. Cookies and Tracking

The App may use cookies and analytics tools to improve performance and personalize your experience.

You can manage or disable cookies in your device settings, though some features may not function properly.

8. International Data Transfers

If you access the App outside Ghana, your data may be processed in countries with different data protection laws. We will take reasonable steps to ensure your information remains protected.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after updates means you accept the revised version.
