Tutor Code of Conduct

Professional standards, ethical guidelines, and responsibilities that all UpSkola tutors must follow to ensure a safe, high-quality learning experience.

1) Professional Standards

  • Maintain a respectful, professional demeanor in all interactions with students, parents, and UpSkola staff.
  • Be punctual and reliable for all scheduled sessions.
  • Dress appropriately for online and in-person sessions.
  • Use clear, appropriate, and respectful language at all times.

2) Academic Integrity

  • Do not complete assignments, exams, or projects on behalf of students.
  • Avoid shortcuts that undermine learning; focus on comprehension and skills.
  • Model and encourage honesty, discipline, and independent learning.

3) Teaching Quality

  • Prepare lesson plans, examples, and materials relevant to each session.
  • Adapt to the student’s level, pace, and learning style.
  • Use engaging, student-centered methods and provide constructive feedback.
  • Keep subject knowledge current and continuously improve pedagogy.

4) Safety & Boundaries

  • Keep interactions strictly professional; personal/romantic relationships are prohibited.
  • Do not request or share unnecessary personal information.
  • For minors, obtain parental/guardian consent for any in-person meetings.
  • Maintain a safe, respectful, distraction-free teaching environment.

5) Platform & Payment Rules

  • All sessions and payments must run through UpSkola—no off-platform deals.
  • Respect booking, cancellation, and rescheduling policies.
  • Report technical issues or disputes promptly to support.

6) Communication Guidelines

  • Respond to messages within 24 hours where possible.
  • Communicate delays/cancellations as early as possible.
  • Use platform messaging/video tools for official communication.
  • Never use offensive, discriminatory, or inappropriate language.

7) Confidentiality & Data Protection

  • Protect student privacy; do not share personal data or academic records.
  • No recording or screenshots of sessions without explicit consent.
  • Comply with all UpSkola data-protection and privacy policies.

8) Reviews & Feedback

  • Accept feedback professionally and use it to improve.
  • Offer constructive feedback to students where appropriate.
  • Do not solicit or pressure students for positive reviews.

9) Compliance & Accountability

  • Abide by all UpSkola policies and community guidelines.
  • Report misconduct, suspicious activity, or abuse immediately.
  • Understand that violations may result in suspension or removal.

10) Commitment to Growth

  • Pursue continuous development in subject mastery and pedagogy.
  • Be open to training, mentorship, and quality assessments.
  • Represent UpSkola with integrity and professionalism.

Acknowledgment

By joining UpSkola, I commit to upholding this Code of Conduct as a tutor. I understand that failure to comply may result in suspension, penalties, or removal from the platform.
